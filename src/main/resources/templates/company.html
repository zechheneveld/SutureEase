About Suture Ease

Suture Ease, Inc., is a San Jose, CA-based medical device company focused on the innovative development and commercialization of devices for laparoscopic surgery. Driven by a passionate group of surgical innovators, the company develops award winning technologies for enhancing laparoscopic procedures. Our flagship device, the CrossBow Fascial Closure System, enables reproduceable trocar site closure by utilizing a proprietary “snare guide” technology in combination with a premium suture passer.

Quality Policy

Suture Ease is dedicated to the design, manufacture, and distribution of medical devices that enhance the efficacy, ease of use, and safety of laparoscopic procedures. Our quality system, quality processes, and quality objectives are designed and maintained to meet customer and regulatory requirements through the focus on continuous improvement.
